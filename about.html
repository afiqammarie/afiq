Target

Focused on Prevention and Universal Care

Our strategy is anchored in SDG Target 3.4, committing to a reduction in premature mortality from non-communicable diseases (NCDs) and the comprehensive promotion of mental health and well-being.

We specifically target the barriers to Universal Health Coverage (UHC), focusing on providing accessible, quality preventative health services and educational resources to vulnerable communities, ensuring that no one is left behind in the pursuit of good health.
